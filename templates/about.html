How to use??

1) Register and Login
2) Update your profile
3) Wanna hold an election?? follow these steps :-
  • create a society using "Add Society"
  • wait for voters to join your society and candidates to participate in your elections
  • Add as many members in your created society by accepting only members you want to
  • approve the member requests you want to for your society
  • start the election whenever you want by clicking on "conduct voting"
  • share any information about the elections in the communicaton channel by clicking on announce
  • end the elections once done and the results will be displayed on the results section
4) A candidate?? follow these steps :-
  • Search the society you want to contest in the search bar
  • send a member request and once the admin of the society starts the election and notifies through the communication channel, contest in it
5) Want to vote?? A voter?? follow these steps :-
  • Search the society you want to vote in the search bar and send a member request
  • Once a member and the admin of the society starts the election and notifies through the communication channel, cast your vote
6) Have something to share?? Go to the communication channel of the respective society(once a member) and click on announce
